Bushwick: Where does it rank in terms of centers for Art in NYC?

Where does Bushwick begin? Has Bushwick as a hot spot for art galleries begun to fade? Do the endless waves of gentrification ensure that Bushwick will grow as a hot spot for art?

Along the East Williambsburg (cough...Bushwick) Industrial Park and Bushwick border we see a lot of movement in the art world. The neighborhood has large galleries with spaces like Luhring Augustine and Clearing but still has is a haven for artist run spaces like ZXY Gallery

That is not to mention other artist run spaces deeper in Bushwick such as Underdonk, Paradice Palase Tiger Strikes Asteroid and Mery Gates. On the other hand, galleries have leftt and moved to neighborhoods that they may even say are upgrades in terms of foot traffic, density of nearby art galleries, closeness to collectors, etc.
